How to make enchiladas

Description

A delicious savory meal that the whole family can enjoy.

Ingredient

  • 3/4 cup of oil
  • 14 corn tortillas
  • 2 1/2 cups of enchilada sauce
  • 2 1/2 cups of cheese, lettuce, and ground beef

Directions

  1. First gather your ingredients.
  2. Warm the oil at a very low heat in a skillet.
  3. Place another skillet and fill it with the tortillas to keep them warm
  4. Take a tortilla and dip in the warm oil for 5-8 seconds until the tortilla has become warm and pliable.
  5. Once you're done, place all the tortillas in a warm pan to keep them warm until you're ready to use them.
  6. Place your enchilada sauce in a bowl so that you can dip each tortilla easily.
  7. Now begin the process of dipping each tortilla one by one into the sauce
  8. Place your finished dipped tortilla in a 9- by 13- inch baking dish and begin placing your filler and meat of your choice down the middle of the tortilla.
  9. Fold each tortilla now.
  10. Turn the enchilada over, seam side down, so the tortilla stays closed, and place it in the baking dish.
  11. Repeat this process until you've done this to all the tortillas.
  12. Once coocked gently drench the tortillas with any remaining sauce.
  13. Top it off with the remaining cheese and lettuce.
  14. Heat the oven to 350 F. Let it bake for 20 to 30 minutes unti the cheese is melted.
  15. Once removed from the oven let it cool off for roughly 10 mintures or so.
  16. Voila some homemade enchiladas.
